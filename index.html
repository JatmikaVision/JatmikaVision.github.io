About me

Samuel Agusta Jatmika Manggala or known as Jatmika Gala was born in Pontianak at Aug 24th, 1999. He envolved in a lot of projects of film making, video clips, profile video, and photography since he studied photography and videography in Jogja Film Academy. His first debut as cameramen and editor was in “Incang-Inceng” (short movie, 2017, Hompympaa Artworks & Cultural Office Yogyakarta) and “Rong” (short movie, 2018, Hompympaa Artworks & Cultural Office Yogyakarta). Apart from his activities as filmmaker , He also has a hobby with old cameras.
